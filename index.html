About Retema

We offer premium, secure student living at Maungani, just next to University of Venda Maungani Gate. Every unit includes in-suite stoves, fridges, laundry sites, tuckshop, parking, 24/7 CCTV, tight security, communal kitchen, unlimited WiFi, and continuous hot water.

Whether funded by NSFAS or private, we ensure comfort and academic focus. Our residence is designed to provide the perfect balance between studying and relaxation.
